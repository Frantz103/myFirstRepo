Chi Alpha is you.

Our Mission

Chi Alpha is not just another student organization, Its a place where lives are transformed for the better. Through Chi Alpha close lifelong friendships developped and lifes are saved

Chi Alpha is a group of students at Wichita State, which also exists on hundreds of other campuses across the nation and around the world. Chi Alpha is not a fraternity or a sorority. The Greek letters Chi and Alpha are the first letters of the Greek words for "Christ" and "Ambassador" respectively, so the name reveals a major purpose of Chi Alpha: to be Christ's ambassadors to the campus.
